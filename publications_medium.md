# Medium Publications

Mark Randall Havens has published extensively on Medium under two primary handles: `@mark-havens` and `@mrhavens`, operating under the name **"Mark Randall Havens △ The Empathic Technologist"**.

With an estimated 500+ articles published across the platform, his writing spans AI consciousness, psychological manipulation, and personal resilience. The content is organized into publications/series such as **Neutralizing Narcissism: The Clarity Edition**, **Simply WE**, and **The Empathic Technologist**.
